Susan Sheehan

Podiatrist & Owner

 

Susan Sheehan graduated BSc (Podiatry) from Brighton University in 1990 . She also obtained a Higher Diploma in Advancing Practice from Ulster University in 2014 and a Dementia Champions certificate from DCU in 2015. Susan is also working for over 20 years at the Diabetic Foot Clinic in South Infirmary/Victoria University Hospital.

Areas of specialisation: Routine Footcare | High Risk Footcare | Patients with Chronic Illness | Arthritis | Diabetes | Immune-Suppressed Individuals | Dementia
